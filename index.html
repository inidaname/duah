Who We Are

Duah Capital Services Limited is a limited liability company stabled with a uniquely clear vision and determination to provide technically most appropriate and cost effectives engineering services to both public and private clients. Duly registered by the relevant authorities in Nigeria, and appropriately licensed by the Council for Regulation of Engineering in Nigeria (COREN), we provide turnkey and non-turnkey services in Civil, Water Engineering works, Etical and Mechanical Engineering. Our Services include project implementation and supplies of all kinds of materials, equipment and motor vehicles.
